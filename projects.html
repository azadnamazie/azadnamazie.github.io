Archival Projects



Viet Rainbow of Orange County (VROC) Oral History Project Digital Archive



Through an ACLS Digital Justice Seed Grant, I was brought on as an archival consultant to build VROC's base by formally creating a multilingual digital repository and developing a digital preservation infrastructure for their oral history project. To support the project's overall goal of fostering intergenerational relationships as community building and popular education, I managed and normalized digital files to ensure their integrity and long-term accessibility, implemented a metadata schema with the Homosaurus controlled vocabulary, and conducted privacy assessments for 30 oral history interviews. I then developed a digital stewardship curriculum and trained VROC staff members to increase their capacity for ongoing and future maintenance.

My peer and friend Prach Prasertwit worked on the front-end web development and Devonte Johnson was the audio/video editor. We met regularly with the VROC team to discuss updates and synchronize workflows.
