Plays


LOCAL PROPHETS: A Play of Near-Collisions
The Joy Who Lived Festival (2025)
McCadden Place Theatre
Director: Graham C. King
Starring Blaire Battle, Maze Felix, and Ari Fromm

Two trans friends meet at the Vermont/Beverly bus stop during ungodly hours of the day. Over time, the intersection of their lives becomes the space where bodies, destinities, and desires, almost-- but never quite--collide.

Starring Gigi (Germain) Arroyo & Vico Ortiz

In the afterlife, a writer seeks the aid of an enigmatic jinn to find their long-lost transcestor.
